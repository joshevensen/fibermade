# Stack

## Hosting & Infrastructure

- **Digital Ocean** — Cloud hosting and managed database with automated backups. Also use Uptime for monitoring the uptime of my pages.
https://docs.digitalocean.com/platform/

- **Laravel Forge** — Server provisioning, deployment, and SSL certificate management.
https://forge.laravel.com/docs/introduction


## Email

- **Postmark** — Transactional email (order confirmations, invitations, password resets).
https://postmarkapp.com/developer

- **MailerLite** — Marketing email (newsletters, announcements, onboarding sequences).
https://www.mailerlite.com/help

## Monitoring

- **Sentry** — Error tracking and application health monitoring.
https://docs.sentry.io/

## Payments

- **Stripe** — Payment processing for wholesale orders.
https://docs.stripe.com/
